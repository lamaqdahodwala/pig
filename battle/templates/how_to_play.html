How to play

The aim of the game is simple: In 5 rounds, get the highest score you can.


Each play you can choose to roll the dice or end the round yourself. If you roll a 6 at any time, you get 0 points for the round and the round is over. Otherwise, the roll is added to your points this round. If you end the round yourself, you safely deposit your points and the next round begins
